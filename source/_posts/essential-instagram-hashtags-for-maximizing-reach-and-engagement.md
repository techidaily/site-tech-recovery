## Essential Instagram Hashtags for Maximizing Reach and Engagement

Close 

 For many[ Instagram](https://www.lifewire.com/things-you-should-definitely-be-doing-on-instagram-3485874) users, gaining likes and[ followers](https://www.lifewire.com/get-instagram-followers-3485845) is essential. One fundamental way to add to your posts' visibility is to[ tag](https://www.lifewire.com/define-tagging-3486207) them with relevant[ hashtags](https://www.lifewire.com/track-instagram-hashtags-3485852) so that users browsing keywords with Instagram's[ search function](https://www.lifewire.com/instagram-search-for-tags-3485862) will discover your content. Here are some popular tags that will help your Instagram posts gain a bigger audience.

##  How Hashtags Work to Boost Your Presence 

 If you post a photo of your sleeping dog and add the hashtag "#dog" in your caption, other users who search #dog may come across your photo and post, like it and comment on it, or even start following you. If you consistently add relevant and popular hashtags, you can keep your content at the top of search results, boost your chances of gaining likes and followers, and even land on[ Instagram's Explore page](https://www.lifewire.com/how-to-get-on-the-instagram-explore-page-4795878) .

### #\[City Name\] 

 If you want to generate more interest from local followers, try tagging your posts with the nearest large city. For example, if you're near Austin, Texas, add tags such as #austin or #austintx.

## More on Hashtags 

 If you're using multiple hashtags in your Instagram caption, try separating them from the actual caption, so it doesn't look cluttered and hard to read. Type out your caption and then make multiple line breaks, placing the hashtags at the end. Some users put periods at the start of their line breaks to help guide the viewer's eyes down toward the hashtags.

 To keep up with current hashtags, visit[ Top-Hashtags](https://top-hashtags.com/instagram/) and check out what's new and trending.
