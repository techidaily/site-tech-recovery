* A Crafting Table (craft with 4 Wood Planks)  
* A Brewing Stand (craft with 1 Blaze Rod and 3 Cobblestones)  
* 1 Blaze Powder (craft with 1 Blaze Rod)  
* 1 Water Bottle  
* 1 Nether Wart
