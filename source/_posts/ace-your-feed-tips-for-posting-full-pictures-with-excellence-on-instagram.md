##  How to Make a Picture Fit on Instagram 

 Instagram automatically crops posts to an aspect ratio of 4:5 so they don't take up too much space in your feed. Fortunately, the app offers a way to make your photos fit the post preview window, and it's really easy:

1. Choose a photo to post.
2. Tap the**Expand** icon in the lower-left corner of the preview window or pinch your fingers together to zoom out and make it fit.  
 You'll see a white border around the image (or a black border if[ dark mode is turned on in the Instagram app](https://www.lifewire.com/turn-on-dark-mode-on-instagram-5070875) ).
3. Tap**Next** to continue posting.
