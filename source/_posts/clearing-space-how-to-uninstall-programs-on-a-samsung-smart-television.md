## How to Hide Apps on the Samsung TV Home Screen

 If you can't (or don't want to) delete an app, you can at least remove it from the home menu:

 There may be variations in the steps depending on your TV's model and year, so consult the user manual if the process below doesn't work.

1. Highlight the app you want to remove from the home screen.
2. Press the**down** button on the remote.
3. Select**Remove** , then select**Remove** again in the pop-up confirmation box. The app should no longer appear on the home screen.  
 You can also move the position of the app on the app bar by selecting**Move** .

 FAQ

* How do I find an app installed on a Samsung TV?  
 Look for it in the**Home** screen menu. If it's not there, go to**Apps** , where all of your TV's apps are listed.
* How do I free up space on my Samsung smart TV?  
 Erase apps you don't use. Reset the Smart Hub. On models made after 2019, also try clearing the app cache and data. If all else fails,[reset your TV](https://www.lifewire.com/reset-samsung-tv-4173258) .
