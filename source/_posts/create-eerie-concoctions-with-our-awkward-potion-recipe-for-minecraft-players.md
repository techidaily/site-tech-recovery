 Here are the materials required to brew an Awkward Potion in Minecraft:  

* A Crafting Table (craft with 4 Wood Planks)

* 1 Blaze Powder (craft with 1 Blaze Rod)  
* 1 Water Bottle  
* 1 Nether Wart
