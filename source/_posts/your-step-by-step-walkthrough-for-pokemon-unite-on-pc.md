 BlueStacks is an Android emulator that works on Windows and macOS. You have other options of Android emulators, but BlueStacks is one of the biggest and most popular choices.

 System requirements for BlueStacks aren't very strenuous, but you'll need at least 4GB of RAM (the developers recommend 8GB). Most CPUs will work just fine, unless you have an older, single-core laptop.

## What to Know About 'Pokémon Unite'

 Pokémon Unite is a MOBA-style take on the_Pokémon_ franchise developed by TiMi Studio Group. It's available on Android, iOS, and Nintendo Switch. Since its release in 2021, the game has seen pretty enduring popularity, with over 80 million downloads across all platforms.

 Unfortunately,_Pokémon Unite_ doesn't have a PC version, nor is it available on PlayStation or Xbox. However, you can play the game on PC with an Android emulator like BlueStacks.

 Considering it's a_Pokémon_ game, which is Nintendo IP, a PC version or ports of the game to other consoles are probably unlikely, so an emulator is your best bet if you don't want to play on a phone.
