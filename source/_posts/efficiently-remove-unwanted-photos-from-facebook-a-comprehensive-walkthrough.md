##  How to Delete Your Profile picture 

 Your profile picture is the image that appears at the top of your profile page and as a small icon next to your messages, status updates, likes, and comments. Here's how to delete it.

1. Go to your profile page on Facebook and click your profile image.  
![Deleting a Facebook profile picture.](https://www.lifewire.com/thmb/8TkQnS3bIRCCo2y5GBDm3vG6spo=/1500x0/filters:no_upscale():max_bytes(150000):strip_icc():format(webp)/FacebookDeletePhotos2-7790ff23f31941e5bb7f096c5324409f.jpg)
2. Select**View Profile Picture** .  
![Deleting a Facebook profile picture.](https://www.lifewire.com/thmb/TbVHn-K0t5lYRcYnUMXgf5w2pko=/1500x0/filters:no_upscale():max_bytes(150000):strip_icc():format(webp)/FacebookDeletePhotos3-e36d795b9c3c41349f3d755b45c17c67.jpg)  
 If you want to change your profile image without deleting it, select**Update Profile Picture** . You can choose an[ image you already have on Facebook](https://www.lifewire.com/add-and-manage-facebook-photos-2654322) or upload a new one from your computer.
3. Click the three-dot menu next to your name.  
![Deleting a Facebook profile picture.](https://www.lifewire.com/thmb/Dx3CtgqmLqCmkUxnC95fUwFq9u8=/1500x0/filters:no_upscale():max_bytes(150000):strip_icc():format(webp)/FacebookDeletePhotos4-360d0cc0fcf64b4a808c5bb29f3cb7a0.jpg)
4. Select**Delete Photo** .  
![Deleting a Facebook profile picture.](https://www.lifewire.com/thmb/zc_vnCO_wn2j8Y4p_iZ59uYospM=/1500x0/filters:no_upscale():max_bytes(150000):strip_icc():format(webp)/FacebookDeletePhotos5-221f6dd94c424f8893bdb9900a14e263.jpg)

##  How to Delete Your Cover Photo 

 The Cover Photo is the large horizontal banner image you can display on the top of your profile page. Your profile picture is inset into the center or bottom left of the cover photo.

 It's easy to delete your Facebook cover photo:

1. On your profile page, click your cover photo (the large one behind your profile image).  
 If you want to change your cover photo but not delete it, go to your profile page and click**Edit Cover Photo** . Click**Select Photo** to pick a image already in your account. If you want to upload one from your computer instead, select**Upload Photo** .
2. Click the three-dot menu next to your name.  
![Deleting a Facebook cover photo.](https://www.lifewire.com/thmb/8y60xtc5kfHc5OgKV37CwXS9qaU=/1500x0/filters:no_upscale():max_bytes(150000):strip_icc():format(webp)/FacebookDeletePhotos5.5-db7a4842be4e47ba85866f087036c9df.jpg)
3. Select**Delete Photo** .  
![Deleting a Facebook cover photo.](https://www.lifewire.com/thmb/QQIhG86xdga1HFyqTrIHOC2XrJ8=/1500x0/filters:no_upscale():max_bytes(150000):strip_icc():format(webp)/FacebookDeletePhotos6-ce94a5c4cdb84d8fbd9dae63d312a7e0.jpg)

You can, however, delete individual pictures inside those albums by opening the picture to its full size, clicking the three-dot menu next to the date, and choosing**Delete Photo** .

1. Select**Photos** on your profile page.

 FAQ

* How do I make photos private on Facebook?  
 To make Facebook photos private, upload your photo as usual and then select**post audience** and select**Friends** ,**Friends except** ,**Specific friends** , or**Only me** . For previously published photos, select**More** (three dots) >**Edit Post Privacy** , and choose new privacy settings.
* How do I add photos to an album on Facebook?  
 To add photos to an existing album on the Facebook app, navigate to your photos and select an existing album. Select**Add Photos/Videos** , select a picture, and tap**Done** \>**Upload** . On a computer, select**Add to album** \>**Upload Photos or Videos.**
* Why are photos disappearing on Facebook?  
 If enough people reported the photos as inappropriate, Facebook may have removed the photos. Or you may also have experienced upload issues when uploading your photos; for example, faulty Wi-Fi can stop a Facebook upload from completing.
