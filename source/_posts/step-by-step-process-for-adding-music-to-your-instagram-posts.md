 This article explains how to add music to your[ Instagram story](https://www.lifewire.com/post-a-story-on-instagram-5069910) . The easiest way to add music to your Instagram Story is to use the special Music sticker, which is available within the latest versions of the official Instagram app on iOS and Android devices.

##  Add Music to Instagram Stories With Music Stickers 

 Follow the below steps to add music to your Instagram story using the Music icon or finding it from the Stickers options:

1. Open the Instagram app and tap the**Your story** icon in the top-left corner to create a new Story.
2. Take a new photo or choose a photo or video from your gallery.
3. Tap the**Music** icon.  
 You can also tap**Stickers** \>**Music** to open the same music library. The Stickers icon is the one that looks like a smiley face post-it note.
4. Browse for and tap the music track you want to use in your Instagram Story.  
 You can select play icons to preview songs before adding one to your Story.  
![Using the Music icon to add a track to an Instagram story in the mobile app.](https://www.lifewire.com/thmb/eECemDOu1TRAWwq9LECyUBB45ys=/1500x0/filters:no_upscale():max_bytes(150000):strip_icc():format(webp)/001_how-to-add-music-to-instagram-video-4691372-648ba8d75cd44c8cad53c758a4c4f223.jpg)
5. Tap**15** and choose the length of the track for your Story and tap**Done** .  
 Track length is limited due to copyright restrictions. Instagram Stories are typically less than 15 seconds anyway so this limitation shouldn’t be an issue for many.
6. Drag the white bar to select the part of the song you want to use and tap**Done** when you've made your selection.
7. Tap the**Music** sticker to choose a different display style or hide the information altogether.  
![Setting the duration, clip, and style for music added to an Instagram story via the mobile app.](https://www.lifewire.com/thmb/neGivpHj_hnftFKdulMR2zWlr8c=/1500x0/filters:no_upscale():max_bytes(150000):strip_icc():format(webp)/002_how-to-add-music-to-instagram-video-4691372-a970ebc37a934c43bd181e67c4becd87.jpg)  
 If you select**Add Yours Music** instead of the Music sticker, you can pinch and resize and drag it to your preferred location. This sticker allows friends to add a song of their own to the story by tapping**Add yours** .
8. Add other stickers, GIFs, and text as usual.
9. Tap**Your story** to publish your new Story to your Instagram account.  
 Alternatively, select the arrow at the bottom right for more sharing options on Facebook, with close friends, or via Message.
10. When you're ready to share or publish the Story, tap**Share** .  
![Options for publishing or sharing an Instagram story in the mobile app.](https://www.lifewire.com/thmb/4k8ek_iSJRkAiCUe3KarqByx4SI=/1500x0/filters:no_upscale():max_bytes(150000):strip_icc():format(webp)/003_how-to-add-music-to-instagram-video-4691372-70e075578ac64b8ba4a2a6c8a3447218.jpg)

##  How to Add Spotify Music to an Instagram Story 

 Another option for sharing music to an Instagram story is to use the share link from Spotify. Here's how to do that.

1. Open the[ Spotify](https://www.lifewire.com/what-is-spotify-4685829) app.
2. Select the song you want to share.
3. Tap the ellipses in the upper-right corner.
4. Scroll down the options and select**Share** .

 In addition to allowing you to crop and add filters to videos, Instagram lets you add music to this type of content. The process is similar to adding music to an Instagram Story.

All video posts are published as Reels.

1. Tap**Your Story** or select the**\+ (Plus)** at the bottom of the screen.
2. Take or select a video from your photo library.
3. Tap the**Music** icon.
4. Search for and select a track to add to the video.
